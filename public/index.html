Why this version is stronger

Minimal interface, more meaningful assessment logic

The updated flow focuses on how often concerns appear over the last 2 weeks and whether those concerns are affecting routines, learning, sleep, and relationships.

Smarter Check-ins

Questions now cover sadness, worry, irritability, sleep, attention, social withdrawal, and daily functioning using one consistent severity scale.

Cleaner Experience

The interface is lighter, calmer, and more futuristic, with softer contrast and clearer visual hierarchy across desktop and mobile.

Better Guidance

The result now highlights the top concern areas so caregivers understand what is driving the score instead of seeing only a generic label.

Core workflow

Designed to feel calm from the first screen

1

Create a caregiver account

Parents, teachers, and counselors can sign in and keep each child profile securely organized.

2

Add the child profile

Keep school, age, and context notes together so assessments stay grounded in real daily life.

3

Complete the 2-week assessment

Answer consistent questions that focus on symptom frequency and impact on functioning.

4

Track patterns over time

Review trend charts, risk shifts, and the strongest concern areas before deciding next steps.

Important note

Focused on patterns and functioning

The prototype is more useful when it watches not just feelings, but also sleep, concentration, peer connection, and whether normal school or home routines are getting harder.
