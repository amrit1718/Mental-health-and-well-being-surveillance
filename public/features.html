Platform features

Designed for calm observation, not complicated workflows

The prototype combines a child-friendly interface with practical tools for monitoring, risk awareness, and decision support.

Interactive Assessment

Evaluate sadness, worry, irritability, sleep, concentration, social withdrawal, and daily functioning.

Live Form Validation

Beginner-friendly JavaScript validation helps users submit complete and accurate data.

Chart-based Dashboard

See score trends and risk distribution through responsive Chart.js visualizations.

Caregiver Authentication

Register and log in with JWT-based protection for child records and assessments.

High-risk Alerts

High-risk assessments trigger a visible UI alert and a console-based email simulation.

Simple Recommendations

Recommendations adapt to the assessment outcome and highlight areas for attention.
